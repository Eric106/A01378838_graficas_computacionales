In gaming terms, a "Zerg rush" is when a player is swarmed by a huge number of weak opponents. Any one of the bad guys is easy to take out, but the threat is that they' ll overwhelm you with sheer numbers.

  • Instructions
  • Kill (click) all the robots before they reach the safe area. In order to kill a robot just click it.
  • Every time a robot reach the safe area you will lose one point.
  • Every time you kill (click) a robot you will earn one point.
  • In the screen you will see a timer, once the timer finish you will have the option of restart the game or go back to the menu.
